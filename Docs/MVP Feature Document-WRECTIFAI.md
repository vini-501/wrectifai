**MVP Feature Document**  
**WRECTIFAI Core Service (Diagnosis to Payment)**

The MVP focuses on the core user flow from AI Diagnosis to Service Booking, Payment, and Review.

1. **AI Diagnosis (Basic)**  
* Functionality: Takes user input (text, image, video, or audio) to provide possible issues/services with a short explanation.  
* Guidance: Provides DIY steps only for safe/minor issues; redirects to garages for critical issues.  
* Output: Drafts an initial quotation, suggesting specific spare parts and providing pricing for those parts.  
2. **Quote & Transparency System**  
* Quoting: Allows the user to raise an issue, and garages submit quotes for comparison.  
* AI Price Transparency: Before selection, the app generates a fair repair cost estimate (Price range, Parts cost, Labor cost estimate, and Confidence level) based on vehicle, service, and location.  
* Quote Comparison: Compares shop quotes against the fair price estimate and labels them as Below market, Fair, or Above market.  
* AI Recommendation: AI analyzes submitted quotes and recommends the 'best quotation' based on price, trust score, and other factors.  
3. **Booking & Garage Management**  
* Garage Features: Self-registration, Admin approval, Profile setup (Specializations, hours), Availability/booking slots.  
* Booking Flow: User selects a quote, books an appointment (with options for self check-in or home pickup).  
* Management: Garage can manage appointments and Accept/Reject Bookings.  
* Communication: User can call the shop directly.  
4. **In-App Payments & Monetization**  
* Mandatory Payment: All payments are processed in-app (Card, Apple Pay, Google Pay).  
* Monetization: The platform earns revenue via a commission per booking.  
* History: Generate invoice/receipt and view payment history.  
5. **Ratings, Trust & Transparency System**  
* Review Mechanism: Only verified users (post-booking) can submit star ratings and detailed feedback on Price, Quality, Time, and Behavior.  
* Trust Score: A shop trust score is built and displayed based on Pricing consistency and Complaint rate (in addition to reviews).  
* Reporting: Specific option for users to report overcharging or poor service.
